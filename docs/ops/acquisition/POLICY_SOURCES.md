## Direct editorial and newsletter routes

- Route: <https://webcurios.co.uk/contact/>
- Current fit evidence: the editor explicitly invites links to good things and

  projects and internet culture.

  surprising Index finding, disclose ownership, and send once. Do not write a
  generic marketing pitch.

- About/editorial fit: <https://theawesomer.com/about/>
- Disclosure/editorial policy: <https://theawesomer.com/disclosure/>

  editorial tips currently go to `help@boingboing.net`.
- Constraint: only pitch when the Index contains an original, defensible story
  or the utility is exceptional; disclose ownership and commercial interest.

## Launch and community routes

### Product Hunt

- Launch guide: <https://www.producthunt.com/launch>
- Sharing rules:
  <https://help.producthunt.com/en/articles/2690626-how-do-i-share-my-post>
- Current constraint: makers may launch their own product; do not mass-message,
  incentivize, coordinate votes, or directly ask people to upvote. Account
  creation and launch remain approval-gated.

### Hacker News / Show HN

- Route and rules: <https://news.ycombinator.com/showhn.html>
- Current constraint: Show HN is for a non-trivial thing the maker built that
  people can actually try. Reading material, lists, signup pages, and quickly
  generated one-offs do not qualify. Do not ask friends to upvote or comment.

### Reddit

- Platform spam guidance:
  <https://support.reddithelp.com/hc/en-us/articles/28012014962580-How-do-I-keep-spam-out-of-my-community>
- Current constraint: promotional content is not automatically spam, but each
  community sets its own policy. Repetitive, unsolicited, off-topic,
  link-disguised, cross-community posting and promotional bots are spam. Review
  the exact community rules and the account's genuine participation history
  before drafting a link post.

### X

- Automation rules:
  <https://help.x.com/en/rules-and-policies/x-automation>
- Current constraint: automated novelty/informational posts may be allowed, but
  duplicative posts, misleading links, unsolicited automated replies/mentions,
  bulk DMs, automated likes, and aggressive following are prohibited. API and
  account access remain approval-gated.

### Bluesky

- Community guidelines:
  <https://bsky.social/about/support/community-guidelines>
- Posting API example: <https://docs.bsky.app/blog/create-post>
- Current constraint: no spam, repetitive disruptive posting, manipulation, or
  undisclosed commercial content. Use channel-native copy rather than an X
  duplicate. Account creation and posting remain approval-gated.

## Cross-channel legal/search guardrails

- FTC affiliate disclosure guidance:
  <https://www.ftc.gov/business-guidance/resources/ftcs-endorsement-guides-what-people-are-asking>
- Amazon Associate social disclosure guidance:
  <https://affiliate-program.amazon.com/help/node/topic/GPXFHVYZMTGPUMPE>
- Google spam policies:
  <https://developers.google.com/search/docs/essentials/spam-policies>
- Google outbound-link qualification:
  <https://developers.google.com/search/docs/crawling-indexing/qualify-outbound-links>

Operational interpretation:

- disclose a material relationship clearly and close to the recommendation;
- `affiliate link` alone may not explain that commissions are earned;
- paid placements should use `rel="sponsored"` (or `nofollow` where needed);
- community/UGC links may be marked `rel="ugc"` by the publisher and still be
  valuable for qualified referral traffic;
- never buy ranking links, exchange links at scale, mass-pitch irrelevant sites,
  or disguise destinations.
